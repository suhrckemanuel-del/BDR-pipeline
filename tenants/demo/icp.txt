Acme Analytics ICP Definition
=============================

Acme sells to revenue leaders at B2B SaaS companies in the growth-to-scale
band. We're a fit when the buyer feels pipeline visibility pain *and* has the
data infrastructure to make a revenue intelligence layer worthwhile.

Tier 1 — Strategic SaaS (highest priority)
  - $50M+ ARR
  - 100+ AEs across multiple segments / regions
  - Multi-product or multi-motion (PLG + sales-led, etc.)
  - Salesforce + a conversation intelligence tool already deployed
  - Public RevOps team or named CRO who has spoken at industry events
  - Examples: Series D+ SaaS, recently public, late growth

Tier 2 — Growth SaaS (active prospect band)
  - $10–50M ARR
  - 20–100 AEs
  - Salesforce live, conversation intelligence either deployed or evaluating
  - Identifiable RevOps function (not just a single Sales Ops analyst)
  - Recent funding round (Series B/C) is a positive signal

Tier 3 — Below Threshold (deprioritize)
  - Sub-$10M ARR
  - <20 AEs
  - HubSpot only, no dedicated RevOps function
  - Founder-led sales without operational infrastructure

Disqualifiers
  - Pure transactional B2C
  - Hardware-only sales motions
  - Companies on legacy CRM (other than Salesforce or HubSpot)
  - Government / public sector primary

Buying triggers (boost any tier by one band)
  - New CRO or VP Sales hired in last 90 days
  - Recent layoff round (signals revenue scrutiny)
  - Public miss on quarterly earnings
  - Forecast accuracy mentioned in earnings call or RevOps job postings
  - New conversation intelligence deployment
